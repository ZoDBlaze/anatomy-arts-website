About Anatomy Arts

Anatomy Arts is an educational media project dedicated to explaining the structure and function of the human digestive system through clear, visual content.

The project focuses on gut anatomy, digestive processes, and related biological concepts, presented in an accessible and visually engaging format for learning and awareness.
